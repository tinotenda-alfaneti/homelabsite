The Technology Philosophy

Every technology choice is deliberate. I favor Go for its performance, single-binary deployments, and excellent standard library. React powers rich frontends when needed, while HTMX keeps things lightweight for simpler interfaces. Kubernetes provides the orchestration layer, and I chose MicroK8s for its lightweight design, snap-based installation, and excellent ARM64 support on Raspberry Pi.

Security isn't an afterthoughtâ€”it's baked in from the start. All traffic flows through Cloudflare's zero-trust network, containers run with minimal privileges, images are scanned before deployment, and secrets never touch version control.

Lessons Learned

Building and maintaining production infrastructure teaches lessons that no book or course can convey:

  • How to debug a pod stuck in CrashLoopBackOff at 2 AM
  • Why idempotent deployments save you from catastrophic rollbacks

Every outage is a learning opportunity. Every performance bottleneck is a chance to dive deeper into system internals. Every security vulnerability discovered is a reminder to stay paranoid.
